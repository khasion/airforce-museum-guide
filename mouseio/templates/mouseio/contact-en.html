About the Hellenic Airforce Museum

The Hellenic Airforce Museum, located at the Dekelia Air Base in Tatoi, showcases an impressive collection of aviation artifacts.

Our mission is to gather, preserve, and exhibit the treasures of Greek aviation, study and document its history, and promote the valor and aviation spirit throughout the ages.

The museum offers a fascinating journey through time, where visitors can explore the bravery and glory of our pilots who defended our country's freedom and security.
